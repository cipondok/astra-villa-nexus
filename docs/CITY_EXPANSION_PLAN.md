## Executive Summary

This document outlines Astra Villa Realty's strategic 3-year expansion plan to achieve national presence in Indonesia's property technology market. Starting from Jakarta as our home base, we will systematically expand to nearby cities, achieve regional dominance in Java, and ultimately establish a nationwide footprint.

**Total Investment:** Rp 5.75 Billion (~$360,000 USD)  
**Timeline:** February 2026 - January 2029  
**Target:** 10%+ market share in national online property listings
