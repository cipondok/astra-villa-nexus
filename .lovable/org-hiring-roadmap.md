## Mission

Build a scalable organization that supports AI technology development, marketplace growth, developer partnerships, investor acquisition, and operational execution as ASTRA Villa scales from early-stage startup to regional proptech leader.

## Stage 1: Founder-Led Core Team (Months 0–12)

**Goal:** Build stable product, validate revenue model, achieve product-market fit in Indonesia.

**Team Size:** 5–6 people | **Burn Rate Target:** $15K–25K/month

### Org Chart

### Role Details

| Role | Type | Key Responsibilities | Hiring Priority |
|------|------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Founder / CEO** | Full-time | Platform direction, developer/agent partnerships, investor narrative, fundraising | Existing |
| **Full-Stack Product Engineer** | Full-time | Feature development, database optimization, AI module implementation, deployment infra | P0 — Immediate |
| **UI/UX Designer** | Part-time / Contract | Dashboard & mobile modernization, design system consistency, user research | P1 — Month 1–2 |
| **Property Operations Manager** | Full-time | Listing onboarding, service provider coordination, legal service partnerships, QA | P0 — Immediate |
| **Digital Growth Marketer** | Full-time | Social media campaigns, SEO optimization, investor acquisition funnel, content | P1 — Month 2–3 |

### Founder / CEO Responsibilities Breakdown

| Area | Time Allocation | Activities |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| Product | 35% | Feature prioritization, sprint planning, QA reviews |
| Partnerships | 25% | Developer JVs, agent networks, bank relationships |
| Strategy & Fundraising | 20% | Investor decks, market positioning, financial planning |
| Operations | 10% | Legal coordination, vendor management |
| Marketing & Brand | 10% | Brand narrative, key content, public presence |

### Stage 1 Milestones

- [ ] 50K+ active property listings
- [ ] 10K monthly active users
- [ ] 3+ developer JV partnerships signed
- [ ] Revenue from commissions or subscriptions validated
- [ ] Mobile investor experience rated 4.5+ stars
- [ ] AI scoring accuracy >80% on Indonesian market data

### Hiring Criteria (Stage 1)

- Generalists over specialists — everyone wears multiple hats
- Indonesian market knowledge preferred
- Remote-first with weekly sync cadence
- Equity-heavy compensation to preserve cash

## Culture & Operating Principles

1. **AI-native thinking** — every team member understands how AI drives the platform
2. **Data-informed decisions** — metrics over opinions; A/B test when uncertain
3. **Speed with quality** — ship fast, but maintain platform trust and reliability
4. **Indonesian roots, global ambition** — deep local expertise, international standards
5. **User obsession** — investor success = platform success
6. **Transparency** — open dashboards, shared OKRs, honest retrospectives
