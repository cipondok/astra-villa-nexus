## 1. Project Vision
_Placeholder._ To become Indonesia's most trusted AI-driven real estate and property investment super app, and a global reference for intelligent property ownership.

## 2. Mission
_Placeholder._ Make discovering, financing, buying, managing, and monetizing property radically simpler, safer, and smarter — for every user, from first-time buyer to institutional investor.

## 3. Core Values
- **Trust first** — transparency, verified data, zero dark patterns.
- **Intelligence by default** — every decision supported by AI insight.
- **Long-term thinking** — build for decades, not quarters.
- **User dignity** — no exploitative UX, no personal data misuse.
- **Operational excellence** — enterprise-grade reliability.
- _Add more as approved._

## 4. Long-Term Goals

### 5-Year Goals
- _Placeholder._ Dominant AI property platform in Indonesia; profitable core marketplace; mature investor OS.

### 10-Year Goals
- _Placeholder._ Regional SEA leadership (ID, MY, SG, TH, VN, PH); full super-app across 7 pillars; licensed financial services.

## 5. Unique Value Proposition
_Placeholder._ The only property platform that unifies **discovery + AI intelligence + transaction + financing + legal + management** in a single Indonesia-first, globally-scalable experience.

## 6. Target Users
- Retail property buyers & sellers (Indonesia).
- Individual property investors (domestic and diaspora).
- Institutional investors and family offices.
- Developers and project sales teams.
- Agents and brokerages.
- Vendors and service providers.
- _Segments to be refined._

## 7. Products & Services
- Property marketplace (buy / sell / rent).
- Investor Operating System (portfolio, deals, forecasting).
- AI Suite (valuation, prediction, autopilot, advisor).
- Escrow & payments.
- Legal & documentation tools.
- Financing / KPR advisory.
- Enterprise Support Center.
- _Future: vendor marketplace, community, lifestyle services._

## 8. Business Model
_Placeholder._ Multi-sided platform monetizing transactions, subscriptions, financial services, and enterprise partners. Free core discovery, paid intelligence and transaction rails.

## 9. Revenue Streams
- Transaction commissions (2.5% platform fee; agent split rules apply).
- Secondary market fees (2%).
- Subscription tiers (investor / agent / developer).
- Escrow and payment processing margins.
- Financial services referral & lending economics.
- Vendor marketplace take-rate.
- Premium data / API access.
- Advertising and featured listings (only if brand-safe).

## 11. Brand Principles
- **Obsidian Signal** identity: Black (#0B0B0B), Gold (#C8A96A).
- Typography: Inter / SF Pro body, Americana / Geist Mono display.
- Tone: confident, precise, quietly luxurious — never loud.
- No generic AI aesthetics, no purple gradients.
- Consistent across web, mobile, email, print.

## 12. AI Strategy
- **AI as backbone**, not feature. Every pillar contributes and consumes intelligence.
- Autonomous scoring: ROI, Liquidity, Deals, Market.
- Human-in-the-loop for high-stakes actions (escrow release, capital moves).
- Model drift monitoring and self-healing pipelines.
- Data flywheel: every interaction improves models across pillars.

## 13. Technology Principles
- Modular pillar isolation with shared identity and payments.
- Mobile-first, offline-tolerant where possible.
- Security by default: RLS everywhere, roles in `user_roles`, secrets in vault.
- Edge Functions use `npm:` specifiers, chunked DB ops, defensive limits.
- Design tokens only (`astra-*`); no ad-hoc colors.
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA minimum.

## 14. International Expansion Strategy
_Placeholder._
- Phase A: Indonesia depth (all Tier 1 & 2 cities).
- Phase B: SEA neighbors (SG, MY, TH, PH, VN) with localized legal modules.
- Phase C: Middle East & AU/NZ for diaspora capital flows.
- Phase D: Global institutional data & API layer.

## 15. Success Metrics
- MAU, pillar-adoption per user, retention (D30/D90).
- GMV, take-rate, net revenue per user.
- AI accuracy (valuation MAPE, deal-close prediction).
- Support CSAT, time-to-resolution.
- Platform health score (≥65 warning, ≥55 freeze).
- NPS.
