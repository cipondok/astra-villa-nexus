# 04 — ASTRA Villa Property Roadmap

Phased plan from foundation to global expansion. Each phase lists Objectives, Major Features, Dependencies, and Success Criteria. Dates are intentionally omitted — phases advance when success criteria are met.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation

- **Objectives:** Establish platform architecture, brand system, security baseline, and governance.
- **Major Features:**
  - Design system (Obsidian Signal, `astra-*` tokens).
  - Auth, roles (`user_roles`), RLS baseline.
  - Core schema (identity, property, deal).
  - Governance docs (this folder) + Technical Blueprint.
- **Dependencies:** None.
- **Success Criteria:** Secure auth, working RLS, brand consistent across surfaces, governance adopted.

## Phase 2 — MVP

- **Objectives:** Ship a usable property marketplace for Indonesia.
- **Major Features:** Listings, search, property detail, favorites, agent profiles, basic messaging, basic KPR calculator.
- **Dependencies:** Phase 1.
- **Success Criteria:** Real users listing and inquiring; core funnels measurable.

## Phase 3 — Marketplace

- **Objectives:** Transaction-grade marketplace with escrow and payments.
- **Major Features:** Escrow (9-state), Midtrans/PayPal payments, offer negotiation, agent commissions, unit reservations, short-term rental calendar.
- **Dependencies:** Phase 2, payment providers configured.
- **Success Criteria:** End-to-end paid transactions with escrow completion; measurable GMV.

## Phase 4 — AI

- **Objectives:** Ship the AI intelligence backbone across the platform.
- **Major Features:** Autonomous scoring (ROI/Liquidity/Deals/Market), price prediction, deal finder, market intelligence, AI advisor, Autopilot, behavioral brain.
- **Dependencies:** Phase 3 data volume, AI Gateway.
- **Success Criteria:** Model accuracy targets met; adoption of AI surfaces by active users.

## Phase 5 — Investor Platform

- **Objectives:** Full Investor Operating System.
- **Major Features:** Portfolio Command Center, Wealth Simulator, Capital Allocation Optimizer, Liquidity & Hedging Engines, REIT/fund infrastructure, secondary liquidity exchange, Astra token economy.
- **Dependencies:** Phase 4 intelligence + regulated financial rails.
- **Success Criteria:** Institutional-grade retention, measurable AUM/portfolio growth on platform.

## Phase 6 — Global Expansion

- **Objectives:** Expand beyond Indonesia into SEA and select global markets.
- **Major Features:** Multi-country legal modules, multi-currency treasury, localized tax/KYC, regional partnerships, global data/API layer.
- **Dependencies:** Phases 2–5 mature; compliance per jurisdiction.
- **Success Criteria:** Live in ≥3 additional countries with positive unit economics.
