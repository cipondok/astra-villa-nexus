## Revenue Architecture Overview

ASTRA Villa operates a **multi-stream revenue model** combining SaaS subscriptions, marketplace commissions, and premium intelligence services. Revenue scales across three customer segments: individual investors, property developers, and institutional clients.

## Payment Infrastructure

### Current Stack
- **Payment Gateway**: Midtrans (BCA, Mandiri, GoPay, OVO, DANA)
- **Edge Function**: `payment-engine` (create_checkout, process_refund, handle_subscription, generate_invoice, verify_payment, wallet_operation)
- **Subscription Enforcement**: `useSubscriptionType` hook + `<FeatureGate>` component
- **Status**: Infrastructure ready; pending Midtrans key activation

### Future Payment Expansion
- **Stripe**: International payments for regional/global expansion
- **PayPal**: Cross-border investor payments
- **Crypto**: ASTRA token for premium feature access (existing Web3 infrastructure)
